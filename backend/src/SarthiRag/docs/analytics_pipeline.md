## **Pipeline Management & Analytics**

Once a drive is active, Saarthi assists in monitoring progress.

### **The Pipeline View**

* **Stages:** Resume Upload → Shortlisting → Emailing → Rounds (1-N) → Final Selection.  
* **"Go to Next Step" Button:** A manual trigger for HR to advance the candidate group to the next phase of the recruitment funnel.

### **Data Visualization (Analytics)**

* **Funnel Metrics:** Percentages showing candidate progression from application to selection.  
* **Drop-off Analysis:** Identifying at which round candidates are most frequently failing or leaving.  
* **Source Performance:** Tracking if candidates are coming from LinkedIn, Naukri, or direct campus links.
